WELCOME TO JARDIN DU TEMPS

Jardin du Temps is a digital garden

that explores the growth of trees through time.

This website allows you to observe how a tree develops

from its earliest stage to full maturity, and into the future.

TIME FLOWS THROUGH SILENT BRANCHES,

UNSEEN, YET ALWAYS GROWING.

EACH RING HOLDS A MEMORY,

EACH LEAF A FLEETING MOMENT.

WHAT YOU SEE IS NOT JUST A TREE—

BUT TIME MADE VISIBLE.
